Professional Endeavors

I currently work as a Data Science Intern at Woodward, as well as develop software for HappyPlace Lab (an HCI lab at Mines). I've found my niche somewhere between software and machine learning, where I help develop software for and deploy machine learning models into production. I'm also very interested in working in the broad fields of sustainability/greentech.

How I Discovered Computer Science

I came to college with a declared major of mechanical engineering, and was certain it was the path for me based on some of the engineering classes I had taken in high school. However, I took the required Introduction to Computer Science (taught in Python) and something just "clicked". I changed my major, chose a focus area of Data Science, and haven't looked back since!
